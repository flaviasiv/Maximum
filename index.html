about us

You need someone who you can trust and you need someone who will clean your home efficiently and thoroughly. We at Maximum Shine Cleaning Services give you exactly that, and more.

Between work, family and an ever-growing to-do list, there simply aren't enough hours in the day to ensure that your home or office is perfectly clean. That's why Maximum Shine Cleaning Services exists - to give you back some of the precious time that would otherwise be wasted cleaning.
Our specialised cleaning professionals will make sure that your cleaning job is completed as efficiently as possible. We offer many cleaning services in Columbus, Ohio to suit your cleaning needs.

Services

The Maximum Shine team is here to make a difference in your life.

Residential Cleaning

If cleaning your home has become too much and you’re in need of professional residential cleaning, Orlandi's Cleaning is simply the best, most affordable choice in Columbus. When you book a house cleaning with Orlandi's Cleaning you gain access to a team of pre-vetted, professional cleaners who are committed to making your home sparkle and bringing you peace of mind.

Office Cleaning

Office cleaning gives a good first impression to your customers and visitors, as well as enhancing your staff’s physical and emotional wellbeing. Reducing viruses, allergens, dust, and mould makes your team healthier and more productive. Our professional office cleaning staff, vetted, trained and supported by our experienced management team will be pleased to help and take away the hassle away from you, so that your staff can stay healthy and concentrate on their work.

Move-in / Move Out Cleaning

You deserve a fresh, clean start in your new home. But after tackling the process of finding a new home, making sense of all your belongings, packing and cleaning your old home, and hiring a moving company, who has the energy or mind to think about tidying up? The best time to clean your new home is while it's empty, so be sure to add “Call Maximum Shine Cleaning Services” to your move-in and move-out checklist.

Post Construction

Our Post Construction Cleaning Services guarantees a clean house from top to bottom after renovations projects you may have. We will send a team of cleaners to make the house brand new. 

Driveways & Garage Cleaning

Our garage cleaning team will take everything out of your garage, we will then make sure to detail all the small areas where the hardest dirt and bugs hide.
